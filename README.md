#Overview
Specula Principum is an an economy and balance mod with a focus on historical accuracy and multiple interleaved anti-snowballing mechanisms. Where ever possible dynamic solutions are favored over static rules. It has been tested and tweaked through a dozens of observer runs with mid-way and post surveys that focus on historical population growth rates, crown vs. estate powers and wealth.

Where possible, systems that the AI doesn't understand well are minimized in order to ensure reasonably competitive gameplay.
