This mod introduces the modifier Country Size Impact, a basic anti-snowballing measure, that scales with a country's economical base, following a simple logarithmic curve. AI countries follow a more forgiving curve, compared to player countries, to compensate for their general inability. Small player countries recieve small penalties, whereas small AI countries instead receive small bonuses. Increasing economical base gives increasing penalties, following a curve that becomes less steep with higher bases, to aovid impossibly high penalties.  


A player country with an economical base of 500, recieves the following scaled effects:

	Tax Efficiency = -0.1
	Trade Efficiency = -0.1
	Production Efficiency = -0.1
	Food Productivity = -0.1
	Legislative Efficiency = -0.1
	Cabinet Efficiency = -0.1
	Max_control = -0.1
	Stability Investment = -0.1
	Estates Target Satisfaction = -0.1
	Crown Power = -0.1
	Research Speed = -0.1
	Diplomatic Reputation = -1
	Antagonism Received = 0.1
